Bicol Express Recipe

A comforting and spicy Filipino dish made by simmering pork in coconut milk with chilies and shrimp paste. Simple, flavorful, and perfect with rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g pork belly, sliced into cubes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 1-2 cups coconut milk
  • 5-8 chili peppers (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© garlic and onion.
  2. Add pork and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Stir in shrimp paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a boil.
  5. Add chili peppers and simmer until pork is tender and sauce thickens.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with rice.
